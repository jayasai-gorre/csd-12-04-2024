About the Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (Data Science) was established in 2022 with the goal of offering a rigorous B.Tech program in Data Science. Our mission is to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the core concepts and methodologies in Data Science. We aim to develop their leadership capabilities, enabling them to excel in software development, hardware implementation, and research within the realm of Data Science.

The College Academic Council, Board of Studies of the department strives to provide quality education and the most advanced curriculum and syllabus to make the students industry ready and excel in the contemporary business world.

The department boasts a team of highly qualified, dedicated, and research-oriented faculty members. Our faculty members are deeply passionate about research activities in the realm of Data Science and are committed to publishing papers in both International and National journals. Data Science is a multidisciplinary field that involves extracting insights from data to make informed decisions and predictions. It encompasses various domains, including statistics, computer science, and domain-specific expertise. Faculty members in our department are enthusiastic about advancing the frontier of Data Science, leveraging their expertise in computational techniques and domain knowledge. Machine Learning, a key component of Data Science, enables systems to automatically learn and improve from experience without explicit programming. It focuses on developing computer programs that can access data and learn from it autonomously, contributing significantly to the field of Data Science.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in academics and research in Data Science, and to undertake challenges for the betterment of society.

Mission

Our Mission is provide industry-aligned education, instilling ethics, research skills, teamwork, and leadership qualities for national progress in a dynamic global environment.

PEO'S

PEO-I: Graduates will demonstrate proficiency in fundamental Data Science concepts, along with advanced computer science principles.

PEO-II: Graduates will pursue successful careers in the computing industry or pursue advanced studies in Data Science or related fields.

PEO-III: Graduates will exhibit strong teamwork, ethical conduct, effective communication skills, and actively contribute to societal welfare through Data Science applications.

PSO'S


PSO-I: Utilize computing skills to develop software systems meeting industry and societal needs.

PSO-II : Apply data engineering and communication technologies to develop smart and intelligent data science applications.

PO'S


Knowledge : Apply mathematics, science, and data science fundamentals to solve complex problems.
Problem Analysis : Identify, formulate, and analyze data science problems, drawing from research literature.
Design Solutions : Develop solutions considering health, safety, societal, and environmental factors in data science contexts.
Conduct Investigations : Use research methods to analyze data and draw valid conclusions in data science projects.
Modern Tool Usage : Utilize data science tools and IT tools for complex activities, understanding their limitations.
Engineer and Society : Assess societal and ethical issues in data science practice.
Environment and Sustainability : Understand and address societal and environmental impacts of data science solutions.
Ethics : Apply ethical principles and uphold professional responsibilities in data science.
Individual and Team Work : Function effectively in diverse teams and settings in data science projects.
Communication : Communicate effectively with the data science community and society.
Project Management and Finance : Apply engineering and management principles to project management in data science projects.
Life-long Learning : Recognize and engage in lifelong learning amidst technological change in data science fields.
